Shipping Information

All orders include free standard shipping. No minimums, no hidden fees.

  • Processing time: 1-3 business days
  • Standard delivery: 7-15 business days
  • Express delivery: 5-10 business days (where available)

Delivery times may vary depending on your location and local customs processing. You will receive a tracking number via email once your order ships.

💬 Most orders arrive within 8-12 business days. We partner with reliable international carriers to ensure safe delivery.

Order Tracking

Once your order has shipped, you will receive a confirmation email with your tracking number. You can use this number to track your package on the carrier's website.

If you haven't received tracking information within 5 business days of placing your order, please contact us.

Return Policy

We want you to be completely happy with your purchase. If something isn't right, we've got you covered.

  • 30-day return window from the date you receive your item
  • Items must be unused and in original packaging
  • Contact us to initiate a return and receive a return shipping label
  • Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive the item

Damaged or Defective Items

If your item arrives damaged or defective, contact us within 48 hours of delivery with a photo of the issue. We will send a replacement or issue a full refund at no extra cost to you.

Exchanges

We currently do not offer direct exchanges. To get a different item, simply return your original order and place a new one.

Cancellations

Orders can be cancelled within 12 hours of being placed. After that, your order may have already entered processing and cannot be cancelled. Contact us as soon as possible if you need to cancel.
