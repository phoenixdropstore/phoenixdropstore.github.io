Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How long does shipping take?▼

Most orders arrive within 7-15 business days. Processing takes 1-3 business days, and you will receive a tracking number via email once your order ships.

Is shipping really free?▼

Yes! Every order ships free, worldwide. No minimum order required. The price you see is the price you pay.

What payment methods do you accept?▼

We accept PayPal and all major credit/debit cards through PayPal's secure checkout. You don't need a PayPal account to pay with your card.

Can I return an item?▼

Absolutely. We offer a 30-day return policy. Items must be unused and in original packaging. Contact us to start a return and we will guide you through the process.

My item arrived damaged. What do I do?▼

Contact us within 48 hours of delivery with a photo of the damage. We will send a replacement or issue a full refund at no cost to you.

Do you ship internationally?▼

Yes, we ship to most countries worldwide. Delivery times may vary depending on your location and local customs processing.

How do I track my order?▼

You will receive a tracking number via email once your order ships. Use that number on the carrier's website to see real-time delivery updates.

Can I cancel my order?▼

Orders can be cancelled within 12 hours of being placed. After that, your order may already be in processing. Contact us as soon as possible if you need to cancel.

How do you choose your products?▼

We scout hundreds of products every week and handpick only the ones that offer real value. Every item is checked for quality, usefulness, and that "wow" factor before it makes it to the store.

When do you add new products?▼

New drops every week! Follow us on TikTok and Instagram (@phoenixdropstore) to see new arrivals first.
