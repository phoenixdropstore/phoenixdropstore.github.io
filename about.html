About PhoenixDrop

Clever gadgets, curated weekly, shipped free

PhoenixDrop started with a simple idea: there are amazing gadgets out there that make everyday life easier, but most people never discover them. We find them so you don't have to.

Every week, we scout hundreds of products across the kitchen and workspace categories to find the ones that are actually worth your money. If it doesn't pass our quality check, it doesn't make it to the store.

Our mission is simple

Find the best gadgets, price them fairly, and ship them to your door for free.
